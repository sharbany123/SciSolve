Our Story

How SciSolve began and where we're headed

SciSolve was founded in 2022 by a team of researchers and technologists who saw the untapped potential of global collaboration in scientific problem-solving. Frustrated by the silos in academic research and the slow pace of traditional scientific discovery, we set out to create a platform that would connect diverse minds to tackle scientific challenges together.

Our platform brings together researchers, students, and science enthusiasts from around the world to work on real scientific problems posted by universities, research institutions, and companies. By leveraging the power of crowdsourcing and collaborative tools, we're accelerating scientific discovery and making research more accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

To democratize scientific research by creating an inclusive platform where anyone, regardless of their institutional affiliation or background, can contribute to solving meaningful scientific challenges and advance human knowledge.

Our Vision

A world where scientific discovery is accelerated through global collaboration, where the best ideas rise to the top regardless of their origin, and where anyone passionate about science can contribute to solving humanity's greatest challenges.

Since our launch, SciSolve has grown to a community of over 50,000 members from 120 countries, collaborating on hundreds of scientific challenges across all disciplines. Our solutions have contributed to published research, patented technologies, and real-world applications in fields from medicine to climate science.
